DICEMASTER 2000 TABLETOP TOOLS

Two browser-based utilities for running or playing Delta Green (or any percentile / d20 tabletop RPG). No installs, no accounts, no server — everything runs locally in your browser and saves to your device.

📋
Agent Documentation

Delta Green Character Sheet

An interactive DD Form 315 — stats, derived attributes, sanity tracking, skills, bonds, equipment, and injuries, styled as a typewritten dossier.

  • Manage multiple agents from one roster
  • Auto-calculated HP, WP, SAN & Breaking Point
  • One-click post-session skill advancement rolls
  • Autosaves as you type
  • Export/import JSON, export to PDF

Session Tracking

Dice Logger

A manual roll log for tracking what happens at the table — organized by session, with running stats and a CSV export.

  • Unlimited named sessions with history
  • Log dice count, modifier, total & individual values
  • Auto-flags crits and fumbles on d20 rolls
  • Filter to misses only, edit or delete any entry
  • Per-session stats & CSV export

How your data is stored

Both tools save everything to your browser's localStorage — nothing is uploaded anywhere. That means your data stays on whichever device and browser you use, and will disappear if that browser's storage is cleared. Use each tool's export feature (JSON for the character sheet, CSV for the dice logger) to keep backups or move data between devices.
